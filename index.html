Research

Questions I am pursuing in the ocean

My interests sit at the intersection of circulation dynamics, variability, and observation, with an emphasis on turning complex physical processes into clean, interpretable stories.

01

Bio-physical coupling of TIW-scale chlorophyll variability on ENSO

Quantifying how tropical instability wave-scale chlorophyll variability interacts with upper-ocean processes and contributes to the modulation of ENSO-related variability.

02

Mixed layer depth, chlorophyll, and shortwave heating

Investigating how mixed layer depth and chlorophyll associated with tropical instability waves modulate upper-ocean shortwave heating and shape ocean-atmosphere coupling.

03

LLCEs off Western Australia

My undergraduate and master's research focused on long-lived cyclonic eddies off Western Australia, from statistics to energy analysis, especially in the context of the Leeuwin Current system.

04

Observation and cruise literacy

I have hands-on cruise and observational experience and understand shipboard measurements and instruments, even though my current research does not primarily rely on those datasets.
